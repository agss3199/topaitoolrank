How to Choose an AI Marketing Tool

The best AI marketing tool depends on your channel and growth stage:

  • SEO: Look for content briefs, SERP insights, and on-page optimization workflows.
  • Ads: Prioritize rapid variations, hooks, and conversion-focused templates.
  • Social: Choose tools that repurpose content and help maintain a publishing calendar.
  • Automation: Integrations and workflow builders matter most at scale.

Tip: For strong performance, combine AI drafts with real customer insights, proof points, and unique examples.
