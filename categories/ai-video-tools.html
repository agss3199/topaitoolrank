How to Choose an AI Video Tool

The best AI video tool depends on the type of video you need and how fast you need to ship:

  • Short-form: Look for auto captions, clip highlights, and repurposing workflows.
  • Marketing: Templates, brand kits, and easy resizing matter most.
  • Editing: Prioritize timeline controls, transcripts, and quick removals.
  • Generative: Choose tools with prompt control, consistency, and export flexibility.

Tip: Start with a script and a clear style reference—AI video quality improves massively with good inputs.

AI Video Tools FAQ

What is the best AI video tool in 2026?

It depends on your workflow. Reels need clipping and captions, marketing needs templates and brand kits, and generative video needs strong prompt control. Use the “Best for” tags above to choose quickly.

Can AI create videos from text?

Yes. Many AI video tools can generate videos from scripts or prompts, often combining stock footage, avatars, voiceovers, and transitions. Quality improves when you provide clear scripts and style guidance, then refine pacing and edits.
