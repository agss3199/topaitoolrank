How to Choose an AI HR Tool

The best AI HR tool depends on where your HR team spends the most time:

  • Recruiting: Look for sourcing, outreach, and pipeline visibility.
  • Screening: Prioritize structured criteria, explainable scoring, and audit tools.
  • Interviews: Choose tools that help standardize questions and capture consistent notes.
  • People ops: Onboarding automation and performance workflows matter most at scale.

Tip: Always keep a human in the loop—especially for hiring decisions and fairness.
